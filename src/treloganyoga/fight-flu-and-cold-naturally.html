Fight Flu and Cold Naturally

Onion to treat the flu

To prevent the flu:

Buy some onions, chop them and place in bowls around your home. If you work at a desk, place one or two in your office or under your desk or even on top somewhere. Try it and see what happens.

If you've got the flu:

Mince one large onion and place it in a cup. Place cup in a bowl with hot water. Make a cone using a paper, cover the cup with the larger part of the cone, insert small part of the cone into the nostril-breath in through the nostril, breath out through you mouth 2-3 breaths,now make another cone and use it for another nostril. You need to blow both nostrils and continue!

You will cry, you will hate it! Take a rest and continue for 5-8 minutes. 2 or 3 procedures usually enough to kill all germs!

Thieves oil blend

Thieves oil blend made up in equal parts of Clove (Syzygium aromaticum), lemon (Citrus limon), cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum), Eucalyptus radiata, and rosemary (Rosmarinus officinalis). The formula is based on the one used by four thieves in the 15th century who robbed plague victims whilst being protected by this blend.

5 drops of each oil to 50ml of carrier oil (almond, jojoba) rub on your hands or soles of the feet to protect against viruses and colds.
